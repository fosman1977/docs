## Billing Overview

<Info>
All figures shown are exclusive of VAT.
</Info>

Your billing dashboard shows financial metrics sourced from LEX payment reports (or estimated from Xero contributions when LEX data is unavailable), including:

- Year-to-date fees billed
- Fees received (collected)
- Outstanding fees (aged debt)
- Fees in pipeline
- Comparison to annual target

## Key Metrics

### Gross Income

Total fees invoiced for work completed, before any deductions.

### Chambers Contribution

Your contribution to chambers operating costs, calculated based on your contribution tier.

### Net Income

Your gross income minus chambers contribution.

## Target Tracking

If you have an annual target set, the dashboard shows:

| Metric | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Progress | Percentage towards target |
| Remaining | Amount needed to reach target |
| Projected | Year-end projection based on current pace |
| Comparison | Same period last year |

## Viewing Details

Click on any metric for more detail:

- List of invoices making up the total
- Payment status of each invoice
- Ageing analysis of outstanding fees
- Monthly breakdown charts

<Info>
Financial data syncs from Xero regularly. Contact accounts if you spot discrepancies.
</Info>
