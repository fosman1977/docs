## Availability Overview

The platform tracks your availability to help clerks make appropriate lead assignments. This enables:

- Visibility of your schedule for clerks
- Better lead assignment decisions
- Workload balancing across chambers

## Managing Your Availability

Keep your availability current by:

1. Updating your profile with working patterns
2. Submitting leave requests through the platform
3. Marking periods of unavailability
4. Communicating with clerks about schedule changes

## Leave Requests

Submit leave requests through the platform:

1. Navigate to your Profile
2. Click "Request Leave"
3. Select dates and type of leave
4. Submit for approval

## Availability View

Access a view of your availability from the Calendar section. This shows:

- Your marked availability
- Approved leave periods
- Assigned opportunities
