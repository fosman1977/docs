## Dashboard Overview

Your dashboard is your central hub, showing key metrics and actions at a glance.

## Dashboard Sections

### Pending Actions

The top section shows items requiring your attention:

- Leads awaiting your response (accept/reject)
- Opportunity invitations pending response
- Overdue follow-ups or tasks

### Performance Metrics

Key performance indicators include:

| Metric | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Lead acceptance rate | Percentage of leads you accept |
| Opportunity participation rate | Your involvement in BD activities |
| Current engagement score | Your overall engagement rating |
| YTD billing figures | Year-to-date fees |
| Utilisation percentage | How busy you are |

### Upcoming Work

See your upcoming hearings, deadlines, and scheduled matters for the next 7 days.

### Recent Activity

A timeline of recent actions and updates relevant to you.

## Customising Your Dashboard

Some widgets can be collapsed or rearranged. Click the settings icon on any widget to see options.

## Refreshing Data

Dashboard data refreshes automatically every few minutes. Click the refresh icon to update manually.

<Warning>
The dashboard highlights items needing immediate attention in red or with alert badges.
</Warning>
