## Dashboard Sections Explained

### Action Required

Items highlighted at the top need your immediate attention:

| Item | What It Means | Your Action |
|------|---------------|-------------|
| **Lead awaiting response** | Clerk has assigned you a potential instruction | Accept or reject with optional notes |
| **Opportunity invitation** | You're invited to a BD event/activity | Confirm or decline participation |
| **Overdue follow-up** | A task assigned to you is past due | Complete or request extension |

<Warning>
Leads marked **urgent** or **critical** appear with red highlighting. Aim to respond within 24 hours.
</Warning>

### Your Performance

Real-time financial metrics from Xero integration:

| Metric | What It Shows | Example |
|--------|---------------|---------|
| **YTD Income** | Gross fees received this calendar year | Â£187,500 |
| **Work Done** | Value of work completed but not yet billed | Â£42,300 |
| **Aged Debt** | Outstanding invoices owed to you | Â£12,100 |
| **Utilisation** | Percentage of time marked as busy (from calendar) | 72% |
| **Engagement** | Your chambers participation score (0-10) | 8.4/10 |

<Tip>
Click any metric to see a detailed breakdown. For example, clicking "Aged Debt" shows invoices by age bucket (30/60/90+ days).
</Tip>

### This Month Summary

Your lead activity for the current calendar month:

- **Leads Received** - How many leads you've been assigned
- **Leads Accepted** - How many you've accepted
- **Acceptance Rate** - Percentage of assigned leads you accept
- **Avg Response Time** - Average time to respond to lead assignments
- **Instructions** - Leads that converted to actual instructions

### Billing Trend

A 6-month sparkline chart showing your monthly billing pattern. Hover over any bar to see the exact figure. This helps you identify seasonal trends and compare against previous periods.

### Upcoming This Week

Your calendar events for the next 7 days, pulled from Microsoft 365:

- Court hearings and trials
- Conferences and meetings
- Document deadlines
- Opportunity events you've joined
