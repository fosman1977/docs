## What is the Engagement Score?

The engagement score measures your active participation in chambers activities. It's a quarterly metric that reflects your responsiveness and involvement in business development.

## Score Components

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Lead Acceptance" icon="check">
    **50%** of your score

    Percentage of leads you accept out of those assigned
  </Card>
  <Card title="Opportunity Participation" icon="handshake">
    **30%** of your score

    Your participation in BD opportunities
  </Card>
  <Card title="Response Time" icon="clock">
    **20%** of your score

    How quickly you respond to assignments
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Score Ratings

| Rating | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| ⭐⭐⭐ Excellent | Top performers |
| ⭐⭐ Good | Meeting expectations |
| ⭐ Needs Improvement | Below expectations |

## Why It Matters

Your engagement score:

- Influences priority for desirable leads
- Is considered in chambers discussions
- Reflects your commitment to chambers
- Helps identify development opportunities

## Improving Your Score

<Steps>
  <Step title="Respond promptly">
    Respond to leads within 24 hours
  </Step>
  <Step title="Accept when possible">
    Accept leads when you have capacity
  </Step>
  <Step title="Participate in opportunities">
    Join BD activities when available
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log your time">
    Keep your billing entries up to date
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
Engagement scores are reviewed quarterly and shared with management.
</Info>
