## Why Your Profile Matters

Your profile information is used by:

- AI suggestions for lead matching
- Clerks when assigning leads
- Management for practice analysis
- Clients viewing chambers capabilities

## Profile Information

### Basic Details

- Name and title
- Call date and seniority
- Contact information
- Profile photo

### Practice Information

- Practice areas (e.g., Criminal Fraud, Civil Fraud)
- Jurisdictions (e.g., England & Wales, DIFC)
- Languages spoken
- Notable cases and experience

### Financial Settings

- Annual target (if applicable)
- Xero contact mapping
- Contribution tier

## Updating Your Profile

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Profile">
    Go to your Profile page
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit Profile">
    Click "Edit Profile"
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update sections">
    Update the relevant sections
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click "Save Changes"
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Profile Visibility

Different information is visible to different users:

| User Type | Access Level |
|-----------|--------------|
| Clerks | See all profile information |
| Other barristers | Basic info only |
| Management | Full access including financial |

<Warning>
Keep your specialisations updated - this directly affects which leads you receive!
</Warning>
