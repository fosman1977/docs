## Overview

The Messaging Hub keeps you connected with chambers communications:

- **Announcements** - Important updates from clerks and management
- **Polls** - Vote on chambers decisions and provide feedback
- **Direct messages** - Communication about specific leads or matters

## Accessing Messages

Navigate to **Messages** in the sidebar to view your inbox.

### Message Types

| Type | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Broadcast | Chambers-wide announcement from clerks or management |
| Poll | A question requiring your vote |
| Direct | Message about a specific lead, client, or matter |

## Chambers Announcements

When clerks or management send a broadcast, you'll receive a notification.

### Viewing Broadcasts

1. Click the notification or navigate to **Messages**
2. Broadcasts appear with a megaphone icon
3. Read the full announcement
4. Attached documents appear below the message

### What to Expect

Broadcasts typically include:
- Chambers policy updates
- Event announcements
- Important deadlines
- Practice area news
- Administrative notices

<Info>
Broadcasts are sent to specific groups. You'll only see announcements relevant to your role or practice area.
</Info>

## Participating in Polls

Clerks and management may send polls to gather feedback on chambers decisions.

### How to Vote

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the poll">
    Click the poll notification or find it in your messages
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the question">
    Read the poll question and available options
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your choice">
    Click on your preferred option
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit">
    Confirm your vote
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Poll Features

- **Anonymous voting** - Some polls hide who voted for what
- **Deadline** - Polls may have a closing date
- **Results** - View results after voting (if enabled)
- **Change vote** - Some polls allow you to change your vote before closing

<Tip>
Vote promptly on polls - your input helps shape chambers decisions.
</Tip>

## Message History

### Viewing Past Messages

Access your complete message history from the Messages page:

- **All** - Every message received
- **Broadcasts** - Chambers announcements only
- **Polls** - All polls (open and closed)
- **Direct** - Lead and matter-related messages

### Search

Use the search bar to find specific messages by:
- Keywords
- Sender name
- Date range
