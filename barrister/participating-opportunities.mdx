<Note>
How to respond to and participate in business development opportunities.
</Note>

## Viewing Opportunities

Access opportunities assigned to you from:

- **Dashboard** - "Opportunity Invitations" widget
- **Opportunities page** - Full list view
- **Notifications** - Alerts for new invitations

## Responding to an Invitation

### Accepting

1. Review the opportunity details
2. Check the dates against your commitments
3. Click "Accept" to confirm participation
4. The opportunity will appear in your dashboard

### Declining

1. Click "Decline"
2. Select a reason
3. Add any additional notes
4. Confirm your response

## After Accepting

Once you've accepted an opportunity:

- The clerk will provide any necessary briefing materials
- You'll receive notifications with event details
- You may receive preparation reminders
- After the opportunity, you may be asked for feedback

## Tracking Results

Opportunities you participate in appear in your profile and contribute to your engagement score. Any leads generated from your participation are linked for tracking.

<Info>
Even if you can't participate, responding promptly helps clerks plan effectively.
</Info>
