## Performance Overview

Your performance page shows key metrics that contribute to your engagement score and demonstrate your chambers involvement.

## Key Metrics

### Lead Acceptance Rate

Percentage of leads you accept out of those assigned to you.

| Rate | Rating |
|------|--------|
| 80%+ | Excellent |
| 60-80% | Good |
| Below 60% | Needs discussion with clerks |

### Opportunity Participation Rate

Percentage of opportunities you participate in out of those offered.

### Conversion Rate

Percentage of leads you accept that convert to instructions.

### Response Time

Average time taken to respond to lead assignments.

## Trends and Comparisons

Performance data is shown as:

- Current period metrics
- Trend over last 12 months
- Comparison to chambers average (anonymised)
- Year-on-year comparison

## Using Performance Data

Review your performance regularly to:

- Identify areas for improvement
- Track progress over time
- Prepare for development discussions
- Understand your engagement score

<Tip>
Performance metrics help identify opportunities to develop your practice.
</Tip>
