## Key Features for Barristers

### Dashboard

Your dashboard shows at a glance:

- Pending leads requiring your response
- Active matters and their status
- Upcoming calendar events
- Your engagement score
- Recent billing activity

### Lead Response

When assigned a lead:

1. Review the lead details and client information
2. Check for any conflicts of interest
3. Accept if you can take the work
4. Reject with a reason if you cannot

### Opportunities

Business development opportunities like events, articles, and presentations. Participate to improve your engagement score and visibility.
