<Note>
Record your billable time entries directly in the platform. Clerks receive your entries in real-time for invoicing.
</Note>

## Why Log Time Here?

Recording time in the Chambers Platform:

- **Instant visibility** - Clerks see your entries immediately via real-time updates
- **No chasing** - Eliminates back-and-forth for billing information
- **Accurate utilisation** - Your workload data stays current
- **Faster invoicing** - Clerks can process invoices without delays

## Accessing Time Entry

Navigate to **Billing** in the sidebar, then select the **Time Entry** tab.

## Recording Time

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select matter">
    Choose the case or matter from your active cases list
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter details">
    Add the date, hours worked, and description of work performed
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set rate (if applicable)">
    Confirm the hourly rate or select the appropriate fee type
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit entry">
    Click "Submit" to record the entry
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Entry Fields

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Matter | The case this time relates to |
| Date | When the work was performed |
| Hours | Duration of billable time |
| Description | Summary of work performed |
| Rate Type | Hourly rate or fixed fee |
| Amount | Calculated or manual fee amount |

## Entry Status

Your time entries progress through these statuses:

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| Draft | Not yet submitted, only visible to you |
| Submitted | Sent to clerks for processing |
| Approved | Confirmed by clerk, ready for invoicing |
| Invoiced | Included on a client invoice |
| Paid | Payment received |

## Real-Time Updates

When you submit a time entry:

1. Clerks receive an instant notification
2. The entry appears on their billing dashboard
3. They can immediately begin invoice processing
4. You're notified when the entry is approved or invoiced

<Info>
This real-time sync eliminates the traditional delay between logging time and getting invoiced.
</Info>

## Viewing Your Entries

### Recent Entries

See your latest time entries on the Billing dashboard, including status and amounts.

### Full History

Click "View All" to access your complete time entry history with filters for:

- Date range
- Matter
- Status
- Amount range

## Editing Entries

<Warning>
You can only edit entries in Draft or Submitted status. Once approved, entries cannot be modified.
</Warning>

To edit an entry:

1. Find the entry in your time list
2. Click the edit icon
3. Make your changes
4. Save or resubmit

## Tips for Effective Time Recording

<Tip>
Log time daily while details are fresh. Regular entries mean more accurate billing and faster payment.
</Tip>

### Best Practices

- **Be specific** - Include enough detail for the client to understand the work
- **Log promptly** - Record time as you complete tasks
- **Check matters** - Ensure you're logging to the correct case
- **Review regularly** - Check pending entries haven't been missed
