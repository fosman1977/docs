<Note>
Record your billable time entries directly in the platform. Clerks receive your entries in real-time for invoicing.
</Note>

## Why Log Time Here?

Recording time in the Chambers Platform:

- **Instant visibility** - Clerks see your entries immediately via real-time updates
- **No chasing** - Eliminates back-and-forth for billing information
- **Accurate utilisation** - Your workload data stays current
- **Faster invoicing** - Clerks can process invoices without delays
- **Automatic notifications** - Get notified when entries are approved or invoiced

## Accessing Time Entry

Navigate to **Billing** in the sidebar, then select the **Time Entry** tab.

## Recording Time

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select matter">
    Choose the case or matter from your active cases list
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter details">
    Add the date, hours worked, and description of work performed
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set rate (if applicable)">
    Confirm the hourly rate or select the appropriate fee type
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit entry">
    Click "Submit" to record the entry
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Entry Fields

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Matter | The case this time relates to |
| Date | When the work was performed |
| Hours | Duration of billable time |
| Description | Summary of work performed |
| Rate Type | Hourly rate or fixed fee |
| Amount | Calculated or manual fee amount |

## Entry Status

Your time entries progress through these statuses:

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| Draft | Not yet submitted, only visible to you |
| Submitted | Sent to clerks for processing |
| Approved | Confirmed by clerk, ready for invoicing |
| Invoiced | Included on a client invoice |
| Paid | Payment received |

## Real-Time Notifications

The platform uses WebSocket connections to deliver instant notifications at each stage of the time entry workflow.

### When You Submit an Entry

1. **Clerks receive an instant notification** - Appears in their notification centre immediately
2. The entry appears on their billing dashboard
3. They can immediately begin invoice processing

### When Your Entry is Processed

You receive notifications when:

| Event | Notification |
|-------|--------------|
| **Approved** | "Your time entry for [Matter] has been approved" |
| **Invoiced** | "Your time entry for [Matter] has been included on invoice #[Number]" |
| **Paid** | "Payment received for invoice #[Number]" |

<Info>
Notifications are delivered in real-time via WebSocket when you're online, and queued for delivery when you next log in if you're offline.
</Info>

### Notification Settings

You can customise which notifications you receive:

1. Navigate to **Settings** > **Notifications**
2. Find the **Billing** section
3. Toggle notifications for:
   - Entry approvals
   - Invoice inclusions
   - Payment confirmations

## WebSocket Events

For developers, the following events are broadcast:

| Event | Direction | Description |
|-------|-----------|-------------|
| `billing:entry:submitted` | Server → Clerks | Barrister submitted new time entry |
| `billing:entry:approved` | Server → Barrister | Clerk approved time entry |
| `billing:entry:invoiced` | Server → Barrister | Entry included on invoice |

## Viewing Your Entries

### Recent Entries

See your latest time entries on the Billing dashboard, including status and amounts.

### Full History

Click "View All" to access your complete time entry history with filters for:

- Date range
- Matter
- Status
- Amount range

## Editing Entries

<Warning>
You can only edit entries in Draft or Submitted status. Once approved, entries cannot be modified.
</Warning>

To edit an entry:

1. Find the entry in your time list
2. Click the edit icon
3. Make your changes
4. Save or resubmit

## Troubleshooting

### Not Receiving Notifications

1. Check your notification settings are enabled for billing events
2. Verify your browser allows notifications from the platform
3. Try refreshing the page to re-establish the WebSocket connection
4. Check if notifications appear when you next log in (queued delivery)

### Entry Not Appearing for Clerks

1. Ensure the entry status is "Submitted" not "Draft"
2. Check your internet connection
3. Try refreshing the page and resubmitting
