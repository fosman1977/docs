<Note>
How to assign leads to barristers for their review and acceptance.
</Note>

## Assignment Options

### Single Assignment

Assign to one barrister who can accept or reject.

### Multi-Assignment

Assign to multiple barristers simultaneously. First to accept wins (or you can choose if multiple accept).

## How to Assign

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the lead">
    Navigate to the lead you want to assign
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Assign">
    Click the "Assign" button
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select barrister(s)">
    Choose from the list or use AI suggestions
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add notes">
    Optionally add assignment notes
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Click "Assign" to send
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What Happens Next

After assignment:
- Barrister receives notification
- Lead status changes to "Assigned"
- Response deadline starts (if configured)
- You can track responses from the lead page

## Assignment Notifications

Barristers are notified via:
- Platform notification
- Email (if configured)
- Dashboard alert

## Tracking Responses

Monitor assignment status:
- **Pending** - No response yet
- **Accepted** - Barrister agreed
- **Rejected** - Barrister declined (with reason)

<Tip>
Use AI suggestions before assigning to ensure optimal matching.
</Tip>
