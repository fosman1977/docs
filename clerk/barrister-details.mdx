## Details Page

Click on any barrister to view their full profile.

## Profile Sections

### Overview
- Photo and basic info
- Current status
- Quick actions

### Practice Areas
- All specialisations
- Expertise levels
- Jurisdictions

### Performance
- Engagement score breakdown
- Acceptance rates
- Response times

### Billing
- YTD figures
- Target progress
- Contribution tier

### Calendar
- Upcoming commitments
- Availability overview

### Leads
- Current assigned leads
- Historical leads
