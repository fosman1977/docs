## Real-Time Time Entries

One of the platform's most powerful features is automatic time entry sync. When barristers log their billable time, you receive it instantly.

### How It Works

1. **Barrister logs time** - They record hours worked on a matter
2. **Instant notification** - You receive a real-time alert
3. **Review & approve** - Check the entry details and approve
4. **Process invoice** - Include approved entries on client invoices

<Info>
Time entries appear on your dashboard within seconds of submission, eliminating the traditional delays in billing workflows.
</Info>

### Processing Entries

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review pending entries">
    Check the time entry queue for new submissions
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify details">
    Confirm matter, hours, and description are accurate
  </Step>
  <Step title="Approve entry">
    Click "Approve" to confirm the entry
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add to invoice">
    Include approved entries when creating invoices
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Enable desktop notifications to be alerted immediately when barristers submit time entries.
</Tip>

## Data Source

Financial data syncs from Xero:
- Invoices and payments
- Aged debt
- Client accounts

Time entry data originates in the platform and syncs to Xero upon invoicing.
