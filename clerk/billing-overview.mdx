<Note>
Access billing and financial information for your barristers.
</Note>

## Billing Access

Clerks can view billing data for barristers they manage:
- Year-to-date fees
- Outstanding invoices
- Target progress

## Key Metrics

| Metric | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Gross Fees | Total invoiced |
| Collected | Payments received |
| Outstanding | Unpaid invoices |
| Pipeline | Estimated future fees |

## Data Source

Financial data syncs from Xero:
- Invoices and payments
- Aged debt
- Client accounts

## Reports

Generate billing reports for:
- Individual barristers
- Team overview
- Period comparisons

<Info>
Some financial details may be restricted to management users only.
</Info>
