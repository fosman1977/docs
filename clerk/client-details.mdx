Understanding client profiles and available information.
</Note>

## Client Profile

Click on any client to view their full profile.

## Profile Sections

### Company Information
- Company name
- Industry
- Size
- Website

### Contacts
- Primary contact
- Additional contacts
- Contact details

### Engagement History
- All leads from this client
- Converted instructions
- Total value

### Barrister Relationships
- Which barristers have worked with them
- Frequency of instructions

### Notes
- Internal notes
- Tags and categories

## Actions

From the client page you can:

- Create a new lead for this client
- View all past leads
- Add notes
- Update information (if not HubSpot synced)
