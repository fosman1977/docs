<Note>
Step-by-step guide to creating leads in the system.
</Note>

## Before Creating a Lead

Gather the following information:

- Client/instructing party details
- Matter description
- Practice area and jurisdiction
- Estimated value and timeframe
- Any special requirements or conflicts

## Creating the Lead

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Leads">
    Click "Leads" in the sidebar, then click "New Lead" button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Basic Details">
    - **Title** - Clear, descriptive title for the matter
    - **Description** - Detailed description of the work
    - **Source** - How the enquiry came in (email, referral, etc.)
    - **Client** - Select existing client or create new
  </Step>
  <Step title="Classification">
    - **Practice Area** - Select the relevant area(s)
    - **Jurisdiction** - Where the matter is based
    - **Urgency** - Normal, Urgent, or Critical
  </Step>
  <Step title="Financial Details">
    - **Estimated Value** - Expected fee value
    - **Fee Type** - Fixed, hourly, or TBC
    - **Notes** - Any fee-related notes
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click "Create Lead" to save. The lead will be created with "Created" status.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## After Creation

The system will:

- Generate AI barrister suggestions
- Show the lead in your pending items
- Allow you to proceed with assignment

<Tip>
The more detail you provide, the better the AI suggestions will be.
</Tip>
