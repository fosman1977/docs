<Note>
How to create business development opportunities in the system.
</Note>

## Before Creating

Gather information about:
- Type of opportunity
- Date and location
- Target audience
- Suitable barristers
- Expected outcomes

## Creating an Opportunity

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Opportunities">
    Click "Opportunities" in sidebar, then "New Opportunity"
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter basic details">
    - **Title** - Clear description
    - **Type** - Event, article, presentation, meeting
    - **Date(s)** - When it takes place
    - **Description** - Full details
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set requirements">
    - Practice area relevance
    - Seniority level needed
    - Number of barristers
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click "Create Opportunity"
  </Step>
</Steps>

## After Creation

Once created you can:
- Assign barristers
- Send invitations
- Track responses
- Add documents
