## CRM Features

The CRM module provides:

- **Client Profiles** - Detailed client information
- **Contact History** - Past interactions
- **Barrister Relationships** - Who has worked with whom
- **Lead History** - All leads from each client

## Data Sources

Client data comes from:
- HubSpot sync (primary)
- Manual entry
- Lead creation

## Key Information

Each client profile shows:

| Section | Information |
|---------|-------------|
| Overview | Name, company, industry |
| Contacts | Key people and their details |
| History | Past leads and instructions |
| Relationships | Barristers they've worked with |
| Notes | Internal notes and tags |

## Sync Status

Data syncs from HubSpot every 15 minutes. Check sync status in the CRM header.

<Info>
Changes made in HubSpot will appear in the platform after the next sync.
</Info>
