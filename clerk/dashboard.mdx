## Dashboard Widgets Explained

### Leads Summary

| Metric | What It Shows |
|--------|---------------|
| **Total** | All leads in the system (excluding archived) |
| **Unassigned** | Leads created but not yet assigned to any barrister |
| **Pending** | Leads assigned but awaiting barrister response |
| **Active** | Leads accepted and being worked on |
| **Conversion Rate** | Percentage of leads that became instructions (last 30 days) |

<Tip>
Click any metric to jump directly to a filtered lead list.
</Tip>

### Action Required

This widget highlights leads needing your immediate attention:

| Alert Type | Meaning | Action Needed |
|------------|---------|---------------|
| **Unassigned** | Lead has no barristers assigned | Assign at least one barrister |
| **Awaiting Response** | Assigned but no barrister has responded | Follow up or reassign if urgent |
| **Multi-Acceptance** | Two or more barristers accepted | Resolve conflict - select one |
| **Stale Lead** | No activity for 7+ days (varies by priority) | Update status or archive |

### Today's Activity

Live counters of lead movements today:

- **Created** - New leads entered into the system
- **Assigned** - Leads assigned to barristers
- **Accepted** - Leads accepted by barristers
- **Closed** - Leads marked as won, lost, or archived

The dashboard updates automatically via WebSocket connection:

- **Lead status changes** appear within seconds
- **New lead assignments** trigger a notification badge
- **Barrister responses** update the pending count immediately
