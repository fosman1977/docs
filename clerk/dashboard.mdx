## Dashboard Overview

The clerk dashboard provides a comprehensive view of current operations and pending tasks.

## Key Widgets

### Pending Leads

Leads awaiting action:
- New leads to assign
- Leads pending barrister response
- Multiple acceptances needing resolution

### Today's Activity

Summary of leads created, assigned, accepted, and converted today.

### Barrister Availability

Quick view of which barristers are available or busy. Identify quiet periods for proactive assignment.

### Upcoming Opportunities

Business development events and activities coming up.

### Performance Metrics

Key metrics including conversion rates, response times, and lead volumes.
