<Note>
Set up delegations so another clerk can cover when you're away.
</Note>

## What Are Delegations?

Delegations allow another clerk to:
- Manage your leads
- Handle your follow-ups
- Respond on your behalf

## Setting Up Delegation

  </Step>
  <Step title="Set dates">
    When delegation is active
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set scope">
    What they can access
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Confirm the delegation
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Delegation Scope

Choose what your delegate can do:
- View only
- Full management access
- Specific barristers only

## During Delegation

When delegation is active:
- Delegate receives your notifications
- Your leads show in their queue
- Actions are logged as delegated

<Tip>
Always set up delegation before planned absences.
</Tip>
