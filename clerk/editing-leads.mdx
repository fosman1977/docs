<Note>
How to edit and update lead information.
</Note>

## When to Edit

Edit leads when:
- Information needs correction
- Details have changed
- Adding more context
- Updating financial estimates

## How to Edit

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the lead">
    Click on the lead to open details
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Edit">
    Click the "Edit" button
  </Step>
  <Step title="Make changes">
    Update the necessary fields
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click "Save Changes"
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Editable Fields

Most fields can be edited:
- Title and description
- Practice area and jurisdiction
- Financial details
- Client information
- Notes and tags

## Restricted Actions

Some fields have restrictions:
- Cannot change client after assignment
- Cannot edit certain fields after conversion
- Original creator is logged and cannot be changed

## Edit History

All edits are logged in the audit trail showing:
- What changed
- Who made the change
- When it was changed

<Info>
Significant changes may trigger notifications to assigned barristers.
</Info>
