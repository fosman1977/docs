<Note>
How to edit and update opportunity information.
</Note>

## Editable Fields

Most opportunity fields can be edited:
- Title and description
- Dates and location
- Type and status
- Requirements

## How to Edit

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open opportunity">
    Navigate to the opportunity
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Edit">
    Click the "Edit" button
  </Step>
  <Step title="Make changes">
    Update necessary fields
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click "Save Changes"
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Notifications

When you edit an opportunity:
- Assigned barristers are notified of significant changes
- Date changes trigger notifications to all participants
- All changes are logged in the audit trail

<Warning>
Changing dates will notify all participants of the updated schedule.
</Warning>
