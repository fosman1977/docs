Understanding follow-up details and how to manage them.
</Note>

## Follow-up Information

Each follow-up shows:

- **Title** - What needs to be done
- **Due date** - When it should be completed
- **Related to** - Lead, client, or opportunity
- **Assigned to** - Who is responsible
- **Priority** - High, medium, or low
- **Notes** - Additional context

## Actions

### Complete
Mark as done when the action is finished.

### Reschedule
Change the due date if needed.

### Delegate
Assign to another clerk.

### Cancel
Remove if no longer relevant.

## Linking

Follow-ups are linked to their source:
- Click through to see the related lead or client
- Context is preserved
- History is tracked
