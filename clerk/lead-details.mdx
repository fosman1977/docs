## Lead Details Page

Click on any lead to open its details page showing all information and available actions.

## Information Sections

### Basic Details

- Title and description
- Practice area and jurisdiction
- Source and referral information
- Seniority preference (KC/Silk, Senior Junior, Junior, or Any)
- Creation date and creator

### Source Opportunity

If the lead originated from a business development activity:

- **Source Opportunity Badge** - Displays "From: [Opportunity Title]" prominently
- **Link to Opportunity** - Click to view the source opportunity details
- **Attribution Tracking** - Connects BD activities to converted work

<Info>
Leads linked to source opportunities help track ROI on business development activities and events.
</Info>

### Client Information

- Client name and company
- Contact details
- Client history with chambers

### Financial

- Estimated value
- Fee type
- Any notes

### Assignment History

- Current assignments
- Past assignments
- Responses received

### Progress Updates

Track the lead's progress with timestamped updates:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Status** | Current stage (Created, Assigned, Accepted, etc.) |
| **Description** | Detailed notes about the progress update |
| **Updated By** | Who made the update |
| **Timestamp** | When the update occurred |

<Tip>
Add detailed descriptions to progress updates to maintain a clear audit trail of the lead's journey.
</Tip>

## Available Actions

  <Card title="AI Suggest" icon="robot">

### Status Changes

- Mark as converted
- Mark as lost
- Re-assign
- Close

### Communication

- Add notes
- Log calls
- Email client (via integration)

## Adding Progress Updates

When updating a lead's status or progress:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Update Progress">
    Select the "Update Progress" button on the lead details page
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select New Status">
    Choose the appropriate status from the dropdown
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Description">
    Enter a detailed description of the progress or reason for the status change
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click "Save" to record the update with timestamp
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Progress Update Best Practices

- **Be Specific** - Include relevant details about conversations, decisions, or next steps
- **Document Reasons** - When marking as lost, explain why (e.g., "Client chose different chambers - fee sensitivity")
- **Track Follow-ups** - Note any scheduled follow-up actions
- **Client Preferences** - Record any client preferences mentioned for future reference

The source opportunity badge:
- Appears prominently below the lead title
- Links directly to the opportunity details page
- Helps identify leads generated from specific BD activities

## Seniority Preference Display

The lead details page shows the seniority preference setting:

| Display | Meaning |
|---------|---------|
| **KC/Silk** | Client requires King's Counsel |
| **Senior Junior** | 10+ years call preferred |
| **Junior** | Under 10 years call acceptable |
| **Any** | No specific seniority requirement |

This preference affects:
- AI suggestion results
- Manual barrister selection filtering
- Match score calculations
