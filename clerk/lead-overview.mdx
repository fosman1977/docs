## Lead Workflow

The standard lead workflow is:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enquiry received">
    Email, phone, or referral comes in
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create lead">
    Clerk creates lead in system
  </Step>
  <Step title="AI suggestions">
    AI suggests appropriate barristers
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign lead">
    Clerk assigns lead to barrister(s)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Barrister response">
    Barrister(s) accept or reject
  </Step>
  <Step title="Selection">
    If multiple accept, clerk selects winner
  </Step>
  <Step title="Outcome">
    Lead converted to instruction or marked as lost
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Lead Statuses

| Status | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Created | New lead, not yet assigned |
| Assigned | Sent to barrister(s) for review |
| Pending Response | Awaiting barrister decision |
| Accepted | Barrister has agreed to take work |
| Rejected | All assigned barristers declined |
| Converted | Lead became an instruction |
| Lost | Lead did not convert |
