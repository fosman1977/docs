The Meetings feature integrates with Plaud.ai recording devices to capture, transcribe, and intelligently process clerk-client meetings. Recordings are automatically synced and can be linked to leads, contacts, and opportunities for enhanced business intelligence.

## Overview

The Meetings page (`/clerk/meetings`) provides:

- **Recording Management** - View and search all synced meeting recordings
- **Transcription Access** - Read AI-generated transcripts of meetings
- **Entity Linking** - Connect meetings to leads, clients, or opportunities
- **Meeting Insights** - AI-extracted summaries, action items, and signals

## Accessing Meetings

Navigate to **Meetings** in the sidebar to access the meetings dashboard.

<Info>
The Plaud integration must be connected before meetings will appear. See [Integration Management](/management/integration-management) for setup instructions.
</Info>

## Dashboard Overview

The meetings dashboard displays:

| Metric | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Total Recordings | All synced meeting recordings |
| Unmatched | Recordings not yet linked to any entity |
| Pending Actions | Meetings with extracted action items |
| This Week | Recordings from the current week |

## Viewing Recordings

### Recording List

The main view shows all recordings with:

- **Title** - Meeting title or auto-generated from date/time
- **Date** - When the meeting occurred
- **Duration** - Length of the recording
- **Status** - Transcription status (Processing, Complete, Failed)
- **Linked To** - Associated lead, client, or opportunity

### Filtering & Search

Use the filter bar to narrow results:

- **Search** - Find by title, transcript content, or participant names
- **Status** - Filter by transcription status
- **Linked** - Show only linked or unlinked recordings
- **Date Range** - Filter by meeting date

### Recording Details

Click any recording to view:

- Full transcription text with speaker identification
- AI-generated summary
- Extracted action items
- Meeting insights (practice areas mentioned, urgency indicators)
- Linked entities

## Syncing Recordings

Recordings sync automatically from your connected Plaud devices. To trigger a manual sync:

1. Click the **Sync** button in the top-right corner
2. Wait for the sync to complete (progress indicator shown)
3. New recordings will appear in the list

<Note>
Sync frequency depends on your Plaud device settings. Recordings typically appear within minutes of upload to Plaud cloud.
</Note>

## Linking Meetings to Entities

Linking meetings to leads, clients, or opportunities creates valuable context for business development and client relationship tracking.

### How to Link a Meeting

1. Find the meeting in the list
2. Click the **Link** button (or the link icon)
3. The Link Modal opens with three tabs:
   - **Lead** - Link to an existing lead
   - **Contact** - Link to a client contact
   - **Opportunity** - Link to a business opportunity
4. Search for the entity by name
5. Click to select and confirm

### Linking Benefits

| Link Type | Benefit |
|-----------|---------|
| Lead | Meeting context enhances AI barrister suggestions |
| Contact | Tracks client engagement frequency and topics |
| Opportunity | Connects BD activities to meeting outcomes |

### Unlinking a Meeting

1. Open the meeting details
2. Click the linked entity badge
3. Select **Unlink** from the dropdown

## Meeting Intelligence

The platform extracts valuable intelligence from meeting transcripts:

### AI Summary

Each transcribed meeting includes an AI-generated summary highlighting:
- Key discussion points
- Decisions made
- Client requirements mentioned

### Action Items

The AI automatically identifies action items:
- Tasks mentioned during the meeting
- Follow-up commitments
- Deadlines discussed

Action items can be converted to clerk tasks with one click.

### Practice Area Detection

Meetings are tagged with detected practice areas based on:
- Legal topics discussed
- Case types mentioned
- Jurisdiction references

This data enriches lead matching and barrister suggestions.

## Troubleshooting

### Recordings Not Appearing

1. Verify Plaud integration is connected (check Admin > Integrations)
2. Ensure your Plaud device has synced to Plaud cloud
3. Try a manual sync using the Sync button
4. Check your Plaud app for any upload errors

### Transcription Stuck on Processing

- Large recordings may take several minutes to transcribe
- Check back after 10-15 minutes
- If still processing, the Plaud service may be experiencing delays

### Linking Search Not Finding Entity

- Verify the lead/contact/opportunity exists in the system
- Try searching with partial names or alternative spellings
- Check you have permission to view the entity

## Privacy & Security

Meeting recordings and transcripts are handled securely:

- **Encryption** - All data encrypted at rest
- **Access Control** - Only authorised clerks can view recordings
- **Audit Trail** - All access and actions are logged
- **Data Retention** - Configurable retention policies apply

<Warning>
Meeting recordings may contain sensitive client information. Ensure you follow chambers' data handling policies when sharing or discussing meeting content.
</Warning>
