<Note>
Send announcements and messages to all chambers members or specific groups with the Messaging Hub.
</Note>

## Overview

The Messaging Hub is a chambers-wide broadcast system that allows you to:

- Send announcements to all members
- Target specific roles (barristers, clerks, management)
- Include attachments and polls
- Track read receipts
- Schedule messages for later delivery

## Accessing the Messaging Hub

Navigate to **Messages** → **Hub** or click the broadcast icon in the message centre.

## Sending a Broadcast

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click New Broadcast">
    Open the compose window for a new chambers-wide message
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select audience">
    Choose who should receive the message:
    - All Members
    - Barristers Only
    - Clerks Only
    - Management Only
    - Custom selection
  </Step>
  <Step title="Compose message">
    Write your announcement with formatting options
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add attachments (optional)">
    Attach documents, images, or files
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add poll (optional)">
    Create a poll for members to vote on
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send or schedule">
    Send immediately or schedule for later
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Audience Targeting

### Role-Based Targeting

| Target | Recipients |
|--------|------------|
| All Members | Everyone in chambers |
| Barristers | All barrister accounts |
| Clerks | All clerk accounts |
| Management | Admin and management accounts |

### Custom Selection

Select specific individuals when you need to reach a subset of members that doesn't match a role.

## Message Features

### Rich Text Formatting

Messages support:
- Bold and italic text
- Bullet and numbered lists
- Links
- Headings

### Attachments

Attach files to your broadcasts:
- Documents (PDF, Word, Excel)
- Images
- Meeting notes
- Policies and guidelines

<Warning>
File size limit is 10MB per attachment, 25MB total per message.
</Warning>

### Polls

Create polls for member feedback:

1. Click "Add Poll" when composing
2. Enter your question
3. Add 2-5 answer options
4. Set whether votes are anonymous
5. Set poll closing date (optional)

Poll results update in real-time as members vote.

## Scheduled Messages

Send messages at a specific time:

1. Compose your message as normal
2. Click "Schedule" instead of "Send"
3. Select date and time
4. Confirm scheduling

<Tip>
Schedule important announcements for Monday morning when engagement is highest.
</Tip>

## Tracking & Analytics

### Read Receipts

Track who has seen your message:
- Total sent
- Total read
- Read percentage
- Individual read status

### Poll Results

View poll responses:
- Vote counts per option
- Percentage breakdown
- Who voted (if not anonymous)
- Export results

## Managing Broadcasts

### Edit or Delete

- **Edit** - Modify a scheduled message before it sends
- **Delete** - Remove a scheduled message
- **Recall** - Remove a sent message (recipients may have already seen it)

### Archive

Old broadcasts are automatically archived after 90 days. Access archived messages from **Messages** → **Hub** → **Archive**.

## Real-Time Delivery

All broadcasts are delivered instantly via WebSocket:

- Members online receive immediate notification
- Offline members see the message on next login
- Push notifications available if enabled

## Best Practices

<Tip>
Keep broadcasts focused and relevant. Overuse reduces engagement with important messages.
</Tip>

### Recommended Uses

- Chambers announcements
- Policy updates
- Event notifications
- Urgent information
- Polls for chambers decisions

### Avoid Using For

- Individual lead discussions (use lead messaging)
- Client-specific matters (use client messaging)
- Personal messages (use direct messages)
