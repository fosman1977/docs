## What is My Leads?

My Leads shows a filtered view of leads that are specifically relevant to you:
- Leads you created
- Leads assigned to barristers you manage
- Leads requiring your attention

## Accessing My Leads

## View Options

### Pending Action

Leads requiring your immediate attention:
- New leads needing assignment
- Multiple acceptances to resolve
- Overdue responses

### Active

Leads currently in progress:
- Assigned and pending response
- Accepted and being worked

### Completed

Historical view of:
- Converted leads
- Lost leads
- Archived leads

## Quick Actions

From My Leads you can:
- Assign pending leads
- Chase responses
- Update statuses
- Generate reports

<Tip>
Set My Leads as your default view for efficient daily workflow.
</Tip>
