Understanding the opportunity details page and available actions.
</Note>

## Details Page

Click on any opportunity to view full details.

## Information Shown

### Basic Information
- Title and description
- Type and status
- Dates and location
- Target audience

### Assignments
- Invited barristers
- Response status
- Confirmed participants

### Activity
- Notes and updates
- Documents attached
- Generated leads

## Available Actions

- **Edit** - Update opportunity details
- **Invite** - Add more barristers
- **Cancel** - Cancel the opportunity
- **Complete** - Mark as done
- **Link Lead** - Connect a generated lead
