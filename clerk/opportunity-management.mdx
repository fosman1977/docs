## What Are Opportunities?

Opportunities are business development activities that can generate new leads and client relationships:

- Events and conferences
- Speaking engagements
- Articles and publications
- Client meetings

## Opportunity Workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create opportunity">
    Log new BD activity
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign barristers">
    Invite relevant barristers to participate
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track responses">
    Monitor acceptances and declines
  </Step>
  <Step title="Execute">
    Manage the activity
  </Step>
  <Step title="Follow up">
    Track any leads generated
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Opportunity Types

| Type | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Event | Conferences, seminars, networking |
| Article | Written publications, blogs |
| Presentation | Speaking, CPD sessions |
| Meeting | Client introductions |

## Managing Opportunities

From the Opportunities page you can:

- View all opportunities
- Filter by type, status, or date
- See barrister participation
- Track generated leads
