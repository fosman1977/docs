## Key Features for Clerks

### Lead Management

Create, assign, and track leads through their lifecycle. Use AI suggestions for optimal barrister matching.

### Opportunity Management

Manage business development opportunities and assign barristers to events, articles, and presentations.

### CRM

Access client information and CRM data. View client history, contacts, and barrister relationships.

### Barrister Profiles

View and manage barrister profiles, specialisations, and availability.
