<Note>
Generate reports to track performance and activity.
</Note>

## Available Reports

### Lead Reports
- Lead volume by period
- Conversion rates
- Source analysis
- Status breakdown

### Barrister Reports
- Utilisation summary
- Performance metrics
- Engagement scores

### Client Reports
- Client activity
- Value by client
- Relationship mapping

### Financial Reports
- Billing summary
- Outstanding fees
- Target progress

## Generating Reports

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Reports">
    Click "Reports" in the sidebar
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select report type">
    Choose from available templates
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set parameters">
    Date range, filters, etc.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate">
    Click "Generate Report"
  </Step>
  <Step title="Export">
    Download as PDF, Excel, or CSV
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Scheduling Reports

Set up automatic report delivery:
- Weekly summaries
- Monthly analysis
- Custom schedules
