Configure your personal settings and preferences.

## Accessing Settings

Click your avatar → **Settings**

## Available Settings

### Profile
- Display name
- Contact information
- Profile photo

### Notifications
- Email notifications
- In-app alerts
- Digest frequency

### Display
- Default views
- Dashboard layout
- Table preferences

### Workflows
- Default assignment options
- Follow-up reminders
- Auto-complete settings

## Notification Preferences

Configure what triggers notifications:

| Event | Email | In-App |
|-------|-------|--------|
| New lead assigned | ✓/✗ | ✓/✗ |
| Barrister response | ✓/✗ | ✓/✗ |
| Follow-up due | ✓/✗ | ✓/✗ |
| Multiple acceptance | ✓/✗ | ✓/✗ |
