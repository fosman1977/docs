View and manage your barrister team.

## Overview

The Team Overview provides a comprehensive view of all barristers you manage, their current status, and workload.

## How to Access

Navigate to: **Dashboard** → **Team** → **Overview**

## Key Features

### Barrister List

View all barristers with key information:
- Pending lead count
- Active matters
- Recent activity
- Practice areas

### Quick Actions

- View full profile
- Check utilisation
- See assigned leads
- Contact barrister

## Tips & Best Practices

- Check team overview before assigning leads
- Note barristers with fewer active matters for proactive assignment
- Review practice areas to match leads appropriately
