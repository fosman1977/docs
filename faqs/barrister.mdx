title: "Barrister FAQs"
description: "Frequently asked questions for barristers"
---

<Note>
Common questions from barristers using the platform.
</Note>

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I respond to a lead?">
    Open the lead from your dashboard or Leads page, review the details, then click Accept or Reject. If rejecting, select a reason.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What happens if I reject a lead?">
    The clerk is notified of your rejection and reason. The lead may be assigned to another barrister.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How is my engagement score calculated?">
    50% from lead acceptance rate, 30% from opportunity participation, and 20% from response time.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I see other barristers' data?">
    You can only see your own personal data. Aggregate/anonymised data may be shown for comparison.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I update my specialisations?">
    Go to your Profile page, click Edit, and update the Practice Areas section.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Why isn't my calendar showing correctly?">
    Ensure your Outlook calendar is up to date. Calendar syncs every 30 minutes.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What are contribution tiers?">
    The percentage of fees you contribute to chambers, which decreases at higher income levels.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I participate in an opportunity?">
    When invited, review the opportunity details and click Accept or Decline.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
