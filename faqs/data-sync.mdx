## Sync Schedules

| Integration | Frequency |
|-------------|-----------|
| HubSpot | Every 15 minutes |
| Xero | Hourly |
| Microsoft 365 | Every 30 minutes |

## Common Issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Client data is out of date">
    HubSpot syncs every 15 minutes. Wait for the next sync or check the last sync time in CRM.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Financial data isn't showing">
    - Check Xero integration status in Admin
    - Verify the barrister has a Xero contact mapping
    - Last sync may have failed - try manual refresh
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Calendar isn't reflecting my availability">
    - Calendar syncs every 30 minutes
    - Check your Outlook calendar is correct
    - Ensure events are not marked private
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="New client isn't appearing">
    - Check the client exists in HubSpot
    - Wait for next sync cycle
    - Verify sync is not paused
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Integration shows 'Disconnected'">
    - Re-authenticate in Admin → Integrations
    - Permissions may have been revoked
    - Check with your administrator
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Manual Sync

Force a sync by going to Admin → Integrations and clicking "Sync Now" on the relevant integration.
