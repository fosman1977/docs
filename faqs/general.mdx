## Platform Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What browsers are supported?">
    The platform supports Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge (latest versions). Internet Explorer is not supported.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I change my password?">
    Click your avatar in the top right, go to Settings â†’ Security, and use the Change Password option.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I use the platform on mobile?">
    Yes, the platform is responsive and works on mobile devices. Some features work best on larger screens.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How often does data sync?">
    Xero financial data syncs hourly. Client and lead data is managed directly in the platform.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Who do I contact for help?">
    For day-to-day questions, contact your clerks. For technical issues, contact support@chambers.com.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is my data secure?">
    Yes. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We are GDPR compliant and undergo regular security audits.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I export my data?">
    Yes. Most views have export options. For full data exports, contact your administrator.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I get help for a specific page?">
    Press **Shift + ?** from any page to open contextual help for that page.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
