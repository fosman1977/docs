## Common Issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="I forgot my password">
    Click "Forgot Password" on the login page. Enter your email and follow the reset link sent to you.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="My password reset link expired">
    Reset links expire after 24 hours. Request a new one from the login page.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I'm getting 'Invalid credentials' error">
    - Check your email is correct
    - Passwords are case-sensitive
    - Your account may not be activated yet
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="My 2FA code isn't working">
    - Ensure your phone time is correct (sync with network)
    - Wait for a new code cycle
    - Use a backup code if available
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I lost access to my 2FA device">
    Contact your administrator with backup codes, or they can reset your 2FA.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="My account is locked">
    Accounts lock after multiple failed attempts. Wait 30 minutes or contact your administrator.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I can't select my role">
    Ensure you have multiple roles assigned. Contact your administrator if the role selector doesn't appear.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
