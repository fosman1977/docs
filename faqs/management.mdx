<Note>
Common questions from management users.
</Note>

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I add a new user?">
    Go to Admin → Users → Add User. Enter their details and they'll receive an invitation email.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I change someone's role?">
    Go to Admin → Users, click on the user, and modify their role assignments.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I view AI usage?">
    Go to Admin → AI Usage to see usage statistics and costs.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I set AI usage limits?">
    Yes, configure limits in Admin → Settings → AI Configuration.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I connect Xero?">
    Go to Admin → Integrations → Xero and follow the authentication flow.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What do the AI insights mean?">
    AI Insights highlight trends, risks, and opportunities based on your data. Click any insight for more detail.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I export audit logs?">
    Go to Admin → Audit Log, set your filters, and click Export.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How often does financial data update?">
    Xero data syncs hourly. Click refresh for manual update.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
