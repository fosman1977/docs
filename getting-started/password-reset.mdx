<Note>
How to reset your password if you've forgotten it or need to change it.
</Note>

## Forgotten Password Reset

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Login Page">
    Navigate to the login page and click the "Forgot Password" link below the sign-in button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Your Email">
    Enter the email address associated with your account. This must be your chambers email address.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check Your Email">
    You'll receive an email with a password reset link. This link expires after 24 hours.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create New Password">
    Click the link in the email and enter your new password. Remember to use a strong password.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Changing Your Password (When Logged In)

1. Click your name/avatar in the top-right corner
2. Select "Settings" from the dropdown
3. Go to the "Security" tab
4. Enter your current password
5. Enter and confirm your new password
6. Click "Update Password"

## Password Requirements

Your password must meet these requirements:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Minimum 8 characters" icon="text-width" />
  <Card title="Uppercase letter" icon="arrow-up" />
  <Card title="Lowercase letter" icon="arrow-down" />
  <Card title="At least one number" icon="hashtag" />
</CardGroup>

Plus at least one special character: `!@#$%^&*`

<Warning>
Never share your password or use the same password across multiple sites.
</Warning>
