## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in with your chambers email">
    Navigate to the platform and enter your chambers email address. First-time users will receive a password setup link.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your active role">
    If you have multiple roles (e.g., Senior Clerk and Barrister), choose which dashboard to access. You can switch roles anytime from the header menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review your dashboard">
    Your dashboard shows pending actions, key metrics, and quick links. Items requiring attention appear at the top with alert badges.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete your first task">
    **Barristers:** Respond to any pending lead assignments. **Clerks:** Review unassigned leads. **Management:** Check the executive summary.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Press **Shift + ?** from any page to access contextual help and keyboard shortcuts.
</Tip>

## Key Features by Role

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="For Barristers">
    - **Lead Response** - Accept or reject assigned leads with optional notes explaining your decision
    - **Performance Tracking** - View your YTD income (e.g., £245,000), work done, and aged debt at a glance
    - **Utilisation Monitoring** - See your daily/weekly utilisation percentage based on time entries and workload
    - **Opportunity Participation** - Join business development events like seminars, articles, or client meetings
    - **Engagement Score** - Track your chambers participation across leads, opportunities, and responsiveness
    - **Contribution Tiers** - Understand your chambers contribution rate (14%/7%/3%/1% based on income)
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="For Clerks">
    - **Lead Creation** - Create leads manually or via CSV import with automatic deduplication
    - **AI-Powered Assignment** - Get intelligent barrister suggestions based on practice area, seniority, and experience
    - **Lead Lifecycle** - Track leads through Created → Assigned → Accepted → Instructed → Completed
    - **Multi-Acceptance Handling** - Resolve situations where multiple barristers accept the same lead
    - **CRM Management** - Maintain client and contact records with relationship mapping
    - **Barrister Workload** - View active matters and workload data to identify capacity
    - **Report Generation** - Create practice development and performance reports
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="For Management">
    - **Executive Dashboard** - View chambers-wide revenue (e.g., £2.4M YTD), cash reserves, and trend charts
    - **AI Strategic Insights** - Read daily AI-generated analysis of financial risks and opportunities
    - **Top Clients Intelligence** - See which clients generate the most work and any aged debt concerns
    - **User Administration** - Create accounts, assign roles, and manage access permissions
    - **Financial Integration** - Configure Xero sync for automated income and contribution tracking
    - **Audit Logging** - Review all system actions for compliance and oversight
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
