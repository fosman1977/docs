## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Complete your first-time login">
    Use your chambers email to log in for the first time
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your role">
    If you have multiple roles assigned, choose the appropriate one
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore your dashboard">
    Familiarise yourself with the layout and available features
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review your quick start guide">
    Read the guide specific to your role for key workflows
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Press **Shift + ?** from any page to access contextual help.
</Tip>

## Key Features by Role

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="For Barristers">
    - View and respond to lead assignments from your personal dashboard
    - Track your utilisation, billing, and performance metrics
    - Participate in business development opportunities
    - Monitor your engagement score and contribution tiers
    - Manage your professional profile and specialisations
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="For Clerks">
    - Create and manage incoming leads from clients
    - Assign leads to appropriate barristers using AI-powered suggestions
    - Track the entire lead lifecycle from creation to conversion
    - Manage client relationships through CRM integration
    - Monitor barrister availability and workload
    - Generate reports and track practice development
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="For Management">
    - Access executive dashboards with real-time KPIs
    - Monitor financial performance and cash reserves
    - Review AI-powered business insights
    - Manage users, roles, and system settings
    - Oversee all operational metrics and analytics
    - Configure integrations with HubSpot, Xero, and Microsoft 365
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
