## What Are AI Insights?

AI Insights analyse your chambers data to identify:
- Trends and patterns
- Potential risks
- Business opportunities
- Anomalies requiring attention

## Types of Insights

### Performance Insights
- Barrister performance trends
- Conversion rate analysis
- Revenue forecasting

### Risk Alerts
- Cash flow concerns
- Utilisation imbalances
- Client concentration risks

### Opportunity Identification
- Growth areas
- Underserved practice areas
- Client development opportunities

## Viewing Insights

Access from:
- Executive Dashboard widget
- Dedicated AI Insights page
- Email summaries (if configured)

## Acting on Insights

Each insight includes:
- Summary of finding
- Supporting data
- Recommended actions
- Links to relevant areas

<Info>
AI Insights are generated daily and updated as new data becomes available.
</Info>
