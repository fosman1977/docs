## Dashboard Overview

The Financial Dashboard shows:
- Revenue metrics
- Cash position
- Outstanding fees
- Forecasts

## Key Metrics

<Info>
All financial figures displayed are exclusive of VAT.
</Info>

### Revenue
- YTD revenue
- Comparison to target
- Year-over-year growth

### Cash Position
- Current cash
- Aged receivables
- Forecast

### Individual Performance
- By barrister
- Target progress
- Contribution tracking

## Data Sources

Financial data comes from Xero:
- Invoices
- Payments
- Contacts
- Accounts

Sync frequency: Hourly

## Reports

### Revenue Summary
Overall chambers revenue.

### Barrister Billing
Individual performance.

### Aged Debt
Outstanding invoices analysis.

### Cash Flow
Current and projected cash position.
