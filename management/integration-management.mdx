<Note>
Configure and manage integrations with external systems.
</Note>

## Available Integrations

### Xero
- Financial data synchronisation
- Invoice and billing information
- Payment tracking
- Barrister revenue data

## Managing Integrations

Navigate to **Admin** → **Integrations**

### Connecting

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select integration">
    Choose Xero from available options
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authenticate">
    Log in to your Xero account
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorise">
    Grant required permissions
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure">
    Set sync options and map barristers to Xero contacts
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Sync Status

Monitor the integration:
- Last sync time
- Records synced
- Errors/warnings

### Troubleshooting

Common issues:
- Authentication expired (Xero tokens expire after 30 days)
- Permission changes
- API limits reached

<Info>
If you experience persistent issues, re-authenticate in Admin → Integrations → Xero.
</Info>
