## Key Metrics

### Participation
- Total opportunities
- Participation rate
- By barrister breakdown

### Effectiveness
- Leads generated
- Conversion rate
- ROI analysis
- Follow-up task completion rates and outcomes

## Tracking ROI

Link opportunities to generated leads:
- Which events produced leads
- Value of generated business
- Cost vs return

## Reports

### Opportunity Summary
Overview of all BD activities.

### Barrister Participation
Who participates most/least.

### Event Effectiveness
Which types work best.

### Trend Analysis
Participation over time.

## Task Outcome Analytics

Track the outcomes of follow-up tasks to understand engagement effectiveness:

| Outcome | What it Means |
|---------|---------------|
| Successful | Contact engaged, goal achieved |
| No Response | Attempted contact, no reply |
| Declined | Contact not interested |
| Partial | Some progress made |
| Rescheduled | Contact requested later follow-up |
| Failed | Could not complete (bounced, wrong contact) |

Use these metrics to:
- Identify which contacts are most responsive
- Measure clerk effectiveness in follow-ups
- Understand pipeline conversion barriers
- Optimise follow-up timing and frequency

## Using the Data

Use opportunity analytics to:
- Plan future BD activities
- Identify engaged barristers
- Allocate BD budget
- Measure marketing effectiveness
- Track follow-up success rates
