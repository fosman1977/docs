## Heatmap Overview

The utilisation heatmap shows:
- All barristers in rows
- Time periods in columns
- Colour intensity = utilisation level

## Reading the Heatmap

| Colour | Utilisation |
|--------|-------------|
| Dark blue | 80%+ (very busy) |
| Medium blue | 60-80% (optimal) |
| Light blue | 40-60% (capacity available) |
| White/grey | Below 40% (quiet) |

## Using the Heatmap

### Identify Capacity
Spot barristers with availability for new work.

### Balance Workload
See imbalances across the team.

### Plan Ahead
Identify upcoming quiet periods.

### Track Patterns
See seasonal or weekly patterns.

## Filtering

Filter the heatmap by:
- Practice area
- Seniority
- Time period
- Custom groups
