---
title: "Barrister Onboarding Checklist"
description: "Complete checklist for new barristers"
---

<Note>
Follow this checklist to get set up on the Chambers Platform.
</Note>

## Before First Login

<Steps>
  <Step title="Receive credentials">
    Get your login email from the administrator
  </Step>
  <Step title="Prepare authenticator app">
    Download Google Authenticator or similar for 2FA
  </Step>
</Steps>

## First Login

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in">
    Use the credentials provided
  </Step>
  <Step title="Change password">
    Set a strong password
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up 2FA">
    Enable two-factor authentication
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Profile Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Complete your profile">
    Add photo, bio, contact details
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set specialisations">
    Add your practice areas
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add jurisdictions">
    Set where you practice
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Xero mapping">
    Confirm financial link is correct
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Learn the Platform

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review dashboard">
    Understand your main view
  </Step>
  <Step title="Learn lead workflow">
    How to accept/reject leads
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore opportunities">
    How BD opportunities work
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review metrics">
    Understand engagement scoring
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Daily Habits

- Check dashboard each morning
- Respond to leads promptly
- Log your time entries
- Review performance regularly
